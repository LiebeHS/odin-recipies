ceviche

Ceviche is a peruvian dish based on shrimps and different types of vegetables like onions, tomato and oyster juice

Ingredients

  • Shrimps
  • Oyster Juice
  • Onions
  • Avocado
  • Species (pepper, salt, garlic powder)

    • Steps

    1. Marinate the shrimps(peeled and washed) with lemon juice and salt
    2. Keep it in the fridge while you chop the vegetables
    3. Add tomatoes, chiles, onion, cilantro and oregano
    4. Let it marinate a couple hours in the fridge
    5. After time, incorporate both shrimps and vegetables into the same dish
    6. Serve
