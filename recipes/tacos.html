Tacos de Carne Asada

Tacos de carne asada are an excellent dish and very easy to make! they're based on beef and tortilla, very taste for those few ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Corn tortilla
  • Lemon
  • Salsa of your preference

Steps

  1. Turn your grill on, add coal to it and clean the grill and then add the raw beef
  2. Once its on, give each side of the beef 5 minutes in direct heat
  3. Once cooked, cut it and serve
  4. Add lemon and salsa as much as you want to
