Caldo de Pollo

This is latino chicken soup! A super healthy dish made of chicken, rice and vegetables, perfect for those cold days when you want something warm to eat :D

Ingredients

  • 300 grams of chicken breast with no skin
  • 2 1/2 liters of water
  • 50 grams of onions
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Carrots
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Perfectly wash the chicken pieces and boil it in a pot with water
  2. Remove all the foam and throw it away
  3. Add the onion and garlic, cook it for 40 minutes or until the chicken is soft
  4. Remove the chicken from the pot and shred it
  5. Add the vegetables, the cilantro and the chicken and let it boil for 5 more minutes
  6. Serve
